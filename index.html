Hospital Transactions Analysis

From having a flat performance in terms of revenue and performance in 2021, the hospital experienced a geometric growth in 2022.

However, a dramatic decline in revenue and overall performance necessitated this project in Power BI to optimize financial and operational insights. The low number of patients in 2023 led to just 30.8% of profit margin. Also, not all procedures were carrried out in the two locations in 2023, whereas, in 2022, all procedures were carried out in both locations.

e-commerce Business Analysis

Our dataset analysis reveals crucial insights: a 5-day average order processing time, 60% standard, and 51% first class shipping at $7 per order.

Fashion dominates sales at 67%, yielding $2.5m profit, followed by home and furniture at 20% with $587k profit. Consumers lead sales at 51%, followed by corporates at 30% and home offices at 18%. The Central region leads with 22% of sales, presenting valuable optimization opportunities. This thorough analysis was conducted using Excel.

LA Crime Data

This crime data from January 2020 to January 2024 in Los Angeles was carried out using Power BI. Los Angeles saw 932,000 crimes, with a peak of 92,000 in January. Wilshire and West LA were the hardest-hit areas. Key crimes included simple battery assault (73,759 cases) and vehicle theft (100,225 cases).

Most crimes occurred from 12 PM to 10 PM. Demographics showed 65.54% male victims, with 36.4% of Hispanic descent. There were 81,000 adult arrests and 745,000 ongoing investigations.

Real Estate

In analyzing the real estate data, I observed that the total sales amounted to $3 billion from 6,000 transactions, with an average sale of $553K and a maximum sale of $1 million.

Top-performing agents included Robert Davis with 287 transactions and Patricia Miller with 233. Sales were nearly evenly distributed among land (35.46%), commercial (32.32%), and residential (32.22%) properties, with California and Texas leading in state-wise sales at $52.5 million and $49.3 million, respectively.
